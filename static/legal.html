Privacy Policy

At ThaiEdit Pro, we value your privacy. Here is how we handle your data:

  • No Storage: Uploaded files (Images, PDFs) are processed in-memory on our server. They are never saved permanently to our hard drives.
  • Temporary Processing: Once you download your result or close the session, the data is wiped from our server's memory.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your personal identity or sell your data to third parties.
